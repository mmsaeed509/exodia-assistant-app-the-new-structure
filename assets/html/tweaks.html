Tweaks Overview

The Tweaks tab will be available soon, Inshallah! This tab is designed to help you easily manage and customize system configurations, including:


  • • Background, Themes, Icons: Customize your visual experience effortlessly.
  • • Polybar: Easily configure your Polybar settings.
  • • RGB Keyboard: Control RGB settings for Predator Edition laptops.
  • • Picom Config: Tailor your Picom configuration for a smoother desktop experience.
  • • Keybinding: Set and modify keybindings to suit your workflow.
  • • BSPWM Configurations: Manage BSPWM configurations and appearance settings.
  • • Auto-Start: Automatically start Exodia Assistant on boot for seamless access.
  • • Auto-Update: Keep your BSPWM configuration up to date with Exodia OS repositories.
  • • And More: Stay tuned for additional features and enhancements!
